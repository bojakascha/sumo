# Multiplayer
- up to 4 players
## Game modes
- Sumo: push each other of the platform, which shrinks/sinks
- Catch: Race around track, trying to catch the other player
- 2vs1 (3vs1): several smaller players vs one large
## Optional Extras
- Pickups: speedup opponent, heavy, reverse opponents controls
  
# Single player modes
- Race: Race around a track on time
- Maze: Travel around a labyrinth, avoid holes
